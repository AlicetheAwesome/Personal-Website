Deep Learning for Ultrasonic Breast Tumor Image Segmentation

Ultrasonic Breast Tumor images (left); Doctor Diagnosis of tumor contour (center); Deep Learning Prediction of tumor contour(right)

Abstract

Breast cancer is the most invasive cancer in women. However, segmentation of ultrasonic breast tumor images is highly dependent on skills and experience, which may lead to subjective diagnosis. In addition, it would be time-consuming for radiologists to review hundreds of images. Thus, in this research, we propose to use U-Net for segmenting ultrasonic breast tumor images.

The Germs in Belly Button Lint

photographed by differential interferance contrast (DIC) microscopy

Abstract

Nowadays, keeping ourselves clean has become a very important issue. However, often times people ignore the lint accumalating in their belly button, allowing a variety of germs to cultivate inside the hole. In this project, we cultivate the germs and sequenced their DNA, and found two types of germs that are most dominant in the belly button.
